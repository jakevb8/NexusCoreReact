SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 character max)
--------------------------------------
Track, assign, and manage your org's resources from anywhere.


FULL DESCRIPTION (4,000 character max)
---------------------------------------
NexusCore puts your organization's resource management in the palm of your hand.

Whether you're tracking physical assets, managing your team, or reviewing
utilization at a glance — NexusCore keeps everything connected and up to date
in real time.

ASSET MANAGEMENT

• Browse your full asset inventory with instant search and pagination
• Create, edit, and retire assets with name, SKU, description, and status
• Assign assets directly to team members
• Import assets in bulk via CSV upload
• Filter by status: Available, In Use, Maintenance, or Retired

TEAM MANAGEMENT

• View all members of your organization in one place
• Invite new members by email with a single tap
• Change member roles (Viewer / Manager) on the fly
• Remove members when they leave

REPORTS & ANALYTICS

• See total asset count and live utilization rate at a glance
• Visual bar chart breakdown by asset status
• Track Available, In Use, Maintenance, and Retired counts

MULTI-BACKEND SUPPORT

NexusCore connects to either the NestJS or ASP.NET Core backend — switch
between them right from the Settings screen. Your preference is saved
automatically across sessions.

SECURE BY DEFAULT

• Sign in with your Google account via Firebase Authentication
• All API calls use short-lived Firebase ID tokens — no passwords stored
• Organization-scoped data: you only see your own org's resources

ROLE-BASED ACCESS

Managers get full create / edit / delete access. Viewers get read-only access.
New users go through an approval flow before accessing org data.

---

Built with React Native (Expo) for a smooth cross-platform experience on both
Android and iOS.
